Frequently Asked Questions

What does MTS focus on?

Madison Tech Society is a professional development organization focused on career preparation, resume building, interview skills, networking, and helping students land internships and full-time roles in tech.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership in Madison Tech Society is completely free for all JMU students.

How often do you meet?

We have weekly meetings every Wednesday from 5:45 PM to 6:45 PM in EnGeo Building, Room 1210. Attendance is mandatory for all members.

What if I can't attend meetings regularly?

Attendance at our weekly meetings is mandatory. We maintain strict attendance policies to ensure all members are engaged and benefit from our professional development curriculum.

Do you teach programming or technical skills?

No, MTS focuses exclusively on professional development skills like resume writing, interviewing, networking, and career planning rather than technical programming instruction.

How do I apply to join?

Fill out our membership application form and if we think you're a good fit, we'll contact you for an interview to discuss your goals and how MTS can help you achieve them.
