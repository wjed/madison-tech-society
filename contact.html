Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming experience to join?

Not at all! MTS welcomes students of all skill levels. We have resources and workshops for beginners as well as advanced topics for experienced programmers.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership in Madison Tech Society is completely free for all JMU students.

How often do you meet?

We have weekly meetings every Thursday at 6:30 PM, plus additional workshops and events throughout the semester.

What if I can't attend meetings regularly?

That's okay! We understand students have busy schedules. You can still participate in events when available and access our online resources.
