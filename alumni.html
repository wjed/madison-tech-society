MTS graduates who have gone on to make an impact in tech and beyond

Will Jedrzejczak

Class of 2026 — Graduated a year early
Founder & First President
Deployment Strategist @ Palantir Technologies

Will graduated from JMU in May 2026 as Valedictorian of the Information Technology major (3.99 GPA, Summa cum laude) — a full year early. He founded Madison Tech Society and led it as President from its inception. Before graduating, he interned as a NatSec Cloud Engineer at AWS and as an Engineering Intern with the DOJ. He now works full-time as a Deployment Strategist at Palantir Technologies in Washington, D.C., while pursuing his M.S. in Computer Science (AI) at Georgia Tech.
