How to Stand Out Without Burning Out

Lesson 9 - Spring Semester

Previous Lesson Next Lesson

Lesson Overview

Get noticed in a sea of applicants without burning out. Learn to showcase soft skills and leadership, differentiate yourself through a blog, content, or community involvement, and build a personal brand that feels authentic.

Personal Branding Differentiation

Discussion Topics & Talking Points

Opening: Standing Out Without Burning Out

Question: "How do you get noticed when hundreds of people apply for the same role?"

  • Popular companies receive 100–500+ applications per role
  • Being qualified isn’t enough—you need to be memorable
  • Standing out doesn’t mean working 80-hour weeks
  • Authentic differentiation beats generic perfection

Reality Check: You can build a strong personal brand and stand out without sacrificing your wellbeing.

What Makes Someone Unforgettable:

  • Clear personal brand and unique story
  • Demonstrated impact and measurable results
  • Authentic passion that comes through
  • Strong online presence that reinforces their brand

Differentiation Through Content and Community

Ways to Stand Out Without Burning Out

Showcase Soft Skills and Leadership:

  • Lead a club, project, or study group
  • Document your learning (blog, LinkedIn, portfolio)
  • Volunteer or mentor others in tech
  • Speak at meetups or present at class

Content and Community:

  • Write about projects and what you learned
  • Contribute to open source or community projects
  • Build in public and share your process
  • Connect with others in your niche
