Resume Building for Tech Roles

Lesson 2 - Fall Semester

← Previous Lesson Next Lesson →

Lesson Overview

Learn how to create a compelling tech resume that stands out to recruiters and hiring managers. This hands-on workshop covers everything from formatting and structure to writing impactful bullet points that showcase your technical skills and projects.

Professional Development Career Materials

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what makes a tech resume effective and ATS-friendly
  • Learn to write strong, quantified bullet points for projects and experiences
  • Master formatting tools like Overleaf, Canva, and Word
  • Participate in hands-on resume review and improvement workshop

Topics Covered

Resume Structure & Format

  • Essential sections: Contact, Education, Experience, Projects, Skills
  • Optional sections: Certifications, Awards, Leadership
  • ATS-friendly formatting best practices
  • Length guidelines (1 page for students/new grads)

Writing Effective Bullet Points

  • Action verb + Task + Result formula
  • Quantifying impact with numbers and metrics
  • Technical skills integration
  • Avoiding common mistakes and weak language

Formatting Tools

  • Overleaf: LaTeX-based professional templates
  • Canva: User-friendly design platform
  • Microsoft Word: Traditional formatting options
  • Google Docs: Collaborative editing features
