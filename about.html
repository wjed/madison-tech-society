About Madison Tech Society

Building the next generation of tech leaders at JMU

What We're All About

Madison Tech Society is a student-run organization dedicated to empowering JMU students through technology education and professional development. We believe in creating an inclusive community where students can grow their technical skills, build meaningful connections, and prepare for successful careers in technology.

Our Focus Areas

  • Professional Development: Resume workshops, interview prep, and career guidance
  • Career Support: Networking opportunities and industry connections
  • Skill Building: Hands-on workshops and technical lessons
  • Mentorship: Peer-to-peer learning and guidance
  • Collaboration: Team projects and group learning experiences
  • Leadership: Opportunities to lead initiatives and mentor others

MTS Community Photo

Our Story

Madison Tech Society was founded with a vision to create a supportive community for JMU students passionate about technology. Our founder, Will Jedrzejczak, recognized the need for a space where students could come together to learn, grow, and support each other's journey in tech.

The Beginning

Will Jedrzejczak founded MTS to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world tech skills

Growing Community

MTS expanded to include regular workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects

Today

A thriving community of students supporting each other's growth in technology and professional development
