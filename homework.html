Submission Guidelines

Attendance Requirement

You must attend the meeting to receive the homework assignment. Homework submissions without meeting attendance will not be accepted.

Due Dates

All homework must be submitted by 11:59 PM on the due date. Late submissions will result in reduced credit.

Quality Standards

Submissions should demonstrate genuine effort and reflection. One-sentence answers or minimal effort will not receive full credit.

Professional Development Focus

All assignments are designed to enhance your professional skills. Take them seriously as they directly impact your career readiness.
